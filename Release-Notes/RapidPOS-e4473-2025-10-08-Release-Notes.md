# Rapid POS e4473 Integration Release Notes  

*Release Date: October 8, 2025*

---

## What's New

### e4473 Cashier Portal:

- Bug Fix: Resolved issue where after e4473 recertification, Section E allowed save without signature.       

### 4473 PDFs:

- Resolved an issue where clients retaining completed 4473's on paper were downloading editable PDFs instead of read-only versions.
  - Now, completed 4473's are downloaded as read-only PDFs, preventing electronic edits after printing, in accordance with ATF requirements. 
- Clients using 4473Cloud for electronic storage may still download an editable PDF to make changes before manually uploading it to 4473Cloud.
